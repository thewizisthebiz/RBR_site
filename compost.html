Compost Collection

Try a Week for Free

Learn the difference you can make by composting

Our compost collection service is a simple, affordable way for organizations to make a positive impact on their community and environment.

Clean. Simple. Fast.

All you need to do is place some of your would-be trash into our bins instead of the garbage. Then, we pickup your containers on a schedule that works for you, and exchange your full bin for a fresh, clean one.

Local Impact

We collect and process all of your material at our facilities and with our partner gardens across Cleveland so you can be sure you're contributing to local food production, reducing water runoff, and cutting emissions both from landfill off-gassing and unnecessary transport.

Measurable Results

Our reporting helps you track the impact you're making, from your kitchen to your community.
