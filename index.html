How is design understood, adopted and applied across Cleveland, OH?

We’re focused on the facets of design that we can share, that are broadly useful and usable, that enrich the thinking and behavior of organizations and individuals.

You don’t need to be a “designer” to have a spot at Design Breakfast. Come out, grab a coffee. Bring a question, bring a story, bring a friend.

  • Conversation

    • The Breakfast

      Our original flavor.

      8AM, the third Friday of every month, we get together for coffee and a bit of conversation. We cover updates on the group's work but do enjoy stretching our brains a bit—the discussions do tend to range!

      Whether you consider yourself a designer, or are looking to learn more, please feel encouraged to join. Bring a question, a book, a difficulty—or just your thoughts. We look forward to seeing you, bright and early.
